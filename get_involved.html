The Vision:

The future prosperity of rural communities hinges on the caliber and focus of empowerment training for the youth. By making these opportunities available, we are not just empowering individuals; we are shaping an entire generation poised to drive positive change in Kenya and beyond. The knowledge and contributions of these empowered minds have the potential to alter the trajectory of not only Kenya but the world at large.

Job Creation for a Better Tomorrow:

Kenya, like many nations, faces the challenge of a large unemployed youth population. Through The Credence Centre, we aim to address this issue head-on by nurturing and creating a cadre of young entrepreneurs equipped with diverse skills crucial for meaningful job creation. Our goal is to foster an environment where innovation and entrepreneurship thrive, ultimately contributing to the economic growth of the nation.

Linking Communities, Empowering Leaders:

EmpowerRural is more than a training program; it is a platform that establishes a vital link between the youth leaders in rural communities and the National Government. This platform provides a space for open dialogue and strategic collaboration, particularly in the realms of DEVOLUTION and Rural Developments. Together, we aim to echo efforts that will uplift and transform rural areas into thriving hubs of progress and sustainable development.

Shaping Values, Building Futures:

Beyond skills and entrepreneurship, our program aspires to empower young minds to create their own worldview. We believe in instilling positive values that will guide their actions throughout their lives. By nurturing a sense of responsibility, leadership, and community, we envision a future where every empowered individual becomes a beacon of positive change in their respective communities.
